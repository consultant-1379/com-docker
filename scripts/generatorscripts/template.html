Documents

The support library is available in: COM, Ericsson Internal Support

To see what documents that have changed from a previous version, please use the Compare function in CAL Store. Updated, new, or removed documents, if any, will be listed:

  1. Tick the boxes in front of the product numbers of the two libraries you want to compare, for example the two latest releases of a Support library.
  2. Click on the Compare-two-libraries icon on the top tool bar. The Compare libraries window opens.
  3. Click on Execute and the Compare Results list will be accessible.
